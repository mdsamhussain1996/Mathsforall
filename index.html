Gradient Descent

Gradient descent finds the minimum of a function by iteratively moving in the direction of the negative gradient. It's the backbone of training neural networks.

w ← w − α · ∇L(w)

where α is the learning rate and ∇L is the gradient of the loss function.

Learning rate α: 0.15
